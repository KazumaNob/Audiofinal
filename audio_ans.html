  • According to Ancient History Encyclopedia, both the Tarascan and Aztec tribes of Mexico believed that dogs could see and would protect them against ghosts: "Both groups buried their dead with dogs who were thought to then serve the soul in the afterlife as both a guide through the underworld and as a protector against ghosts”.
